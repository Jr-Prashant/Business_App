## App Name (My Company)
<p> Welcome to My Company, a comprehensive business app developed using the Objective-C programming language. This app is designed to provide users with easy access to essential information about our business, services, and portfolio. </p>

## Overview

My Company is a feature-rich business app that offers a user-friendly interface and the following key sections:

1. **Home:** A central hub providing an introduction to our business and quick access to other sections.

2. **Services:** Here we can mention the range of services we offer. Each service is detailed with information to help users understand our offerings.

3. **Portfolio:** Showcase your past projects and highlight the expertise and quality of your work. Include visuals and descriptions for each portfolio item.

4. **About Us:** Share the story behind your business. Provide information about your mission, values, and the team behind the scenes.

5. **Contact Us:** Make it easy for users to get in touch. Include directions of company, Call Us, Email Us and links to your social media profiles.

6. **Social Links:** Connect with your audience on various social media platforms. Include links to your profiles to enhance your online presence.

## Key Features

- **User-Friendly Interface:** Navigating through the app is intuitive, ensuring a pleasant user experience.

- **Detailed Services:** Users can learn more about our services, making informed decisions.

- **Visual Portfolio:** Showcase your work through a visually appealing portfolio.

- **About Us Section:** Build trust by sharing your business's story and values.

- **Contact Us:** Allow users to reach out easily with a built-in contact details.

## Technology Used

1. `Objective-C` Programming Langusge.
2. `XCode` Integrated Development Environment (IDE).
3. `UIKit` User Interface.
4. `Git` Version Control.

## Installation
Getting started with the Objective-C Business App is quick and easy:
1. Clone the Repository: git clone https://github.com/Jr-Prashant/Business_App.git
2. Open the project in Xcode and build/run the app.

## Usage
Explore the various sections of the app to learn more about our business, services, and portfolio. Connect with us through the provided contact options and stay updated via our social media profiles.
